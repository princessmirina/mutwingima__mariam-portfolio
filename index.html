About Me

Hi! I'm Mutwingima Mariam, a passionate tech enthusiast with a background in Computer Science and Technology. My journey in tech began with SheCodes, where I learned the fundamentals of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Eager to grow, I joined FreeCodeCamp to dive into full-stack web development. I later expanded into data science through Coursera, explored AWS Cloud Computing with ALX Africa, and honed my data analysis skills on DataCamp. I’m driven by curiosity, problem-solving, and a desire to build smart, impactful digital solutions.
